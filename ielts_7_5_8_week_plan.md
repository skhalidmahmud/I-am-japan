# IELTS 7.5 – 8 Week Structured Daily Plan

**Goal:** Achieve overall Band 7.5, focusing on Reading, Writing, Speaking weaknesses.

---

## Week 1–2: Foundation & Skill Assessment (Daily 2–2.5 hours)
| Section | Activity | Material |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Listening | 30 min: Cambridge IELTS Listening Test 1, focus on keywords | Cambridge IELTS 1–15 |
| Reading | 45 min: Skimming + Scanning practice | IELTS Liz Reading |
| Writing | 45 min: Task 1: write 150 words chart/graph, focus on sentence structure | Grammarly + IELTS Liz |
| Speaking | 15 min: Self-introduction + 3 common topics | Mirror / Record yourself |
| Vocabulary | 15 min: 10 academic words, use in sentences | Academic Word List |

**Notes:** Keep daily log of mistakes; focus on understanding errors.

---

## Week 3–4: Strengthen Weak Areas (Daily 2.5 hours)
| Section | Activity |
|---------|---------|
| Listening | 40 min: IELTS Listening + Predict answers | Cambridge IELTS 2–5 |
| Reading | 50 min: Full Reading test (40 Qs in 60 min) | Focus: speed + comprehension |
| Writing | 50 min: Task 2 essay (250–300 words), grammar + coherence | Grammarly + AI check |
| Speaking | 20 min: Daily Q&A on AI, Hobbies, Study Goals | Partner / AI practice |
| Vocabulary | 15 min: 10 new academic words + collocations | - |

**Tips:** Time yourself in Reading; check grammar + spelling daily in Writing; record Speaking.

---

## Week 5–6: Full Test Practice (Daily 3 hours)
| Section | Activity |
|---------|---------|
| Listening | 40 min: Full section test, note weak question types | Cambridge IELTS 6–10 |
| Reading | 1 hour: Full Reading test | Focus: True/False/Not Given + Matching |
| Writing | 50 min: Task 1 + Task 2 daily, use template | Grammarly check, 1 rewrite |
| Speaking | 30 min: Full Part 1,2,3 simulation | Record & analyze |
| Vocabulary | 15 min: Use words in Writing & Speaking | Review previous weeks |

**Extra Notes:** Start using synonyms + transition words in essays; Speaking: fluency > grammar.

---

## Week 7: Intensive Weak Spot Focus (Daily 3 hours)
- Reading: 60 min → Practice 1 Reading test under strict time
- Writing: 1 hour → Task 1 + Task 2, write + revise grammar mistakes
- Speaking: 30 min → Partner / AI mock test, focus on pronunciation + fluency
- Listening: 30 min → Difficult sections, check keyword strategy
- Vocabulary: 15 min → Daily 10 words → use in writing & speaking

**Extra Notes:** Analyze errors → make mini correction sheet; practice paraphrasing.

---

## Week 8: Mock Exam & Fine-Tuning (Daily 3 hours)
| Day | Activity |
|-----|---------|
| Mon | Full Listening + Reading (timed) |
| Tue | Full Writing Test (Task 1 + 2), evaluate with Grammarly |
| Wed | Speaking mock test (record, check) |
| Thu | Full Listening + Reading |
| Fri | Writing + Vocabulary review |
| Sat | Speaking + Listening weak parts practice |
| Sun | Review + Rest + Reflection |

**Extra Tips:** Mimic real exam environment; focus on time management; record all speaking → correct errors.

---

## Daily Mindset & Motivation
- Mistakes = learning, not fear
- Fluency > Grammar in speaking at first
- Writing → rewrite mistakes daily
- Confidence = success key

---

## Expected Outcome
- Reading → Band 7–7.5
- Listening → Band 7.5
- Writing → Band 7–7.5
- Speaking → Band 7–7.5

**Overall Target:** Band 7.5
